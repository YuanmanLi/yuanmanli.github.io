Biography

I currently serve as an assistant professor with the VCC group at Shenzhen Univeristy. I received my Ph.D. degree from the Computer Science and Engineering Department, the Chinese University of Hong Kong, and B.Eng. degree in software engineering at Sun Yat-Sen University, China. My CV is here.

My research topics include computer vision and machine learning.
